Computer Laboratory Management System

Department of Business & Management Studies — Eastern University of Sri Lanka

A full-stack web application designed to eliminate manual inefficiencies in managing a 75-computer university lab. The system automates scheduling, attendance tracking, maintenance requests, and resource allocation through role-based dashboards for Admins, Lecturers, and Students.

3 User Roles
75 Computers Managed
6+ Core Modules

Role-Based Authentication

✅
Attendance Management

Maintenance Request System
